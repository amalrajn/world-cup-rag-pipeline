encyclopedia As of the 2022 FIFA World Cup , 80 national teams have competed at the finals of the men’s FIFA World Cup . [ 1 ] Brazil is the only team to have appeared in all 22 tournaments to date, with Germany having participated in 20, Italy and Argentina in 18 and Mexico in 17. [ 2 ] Eight nations have won the tournament. The inaugural winners in 1930 were Uruguay ; the current champions are Argentina. The most successful nation is Brazil, which has won the cup on five occasions. [ 3 ] Five teams have appeared in FIFA World Cup finals without winning, [ 4 ] while twelve more have appeared in the semi-finals.
